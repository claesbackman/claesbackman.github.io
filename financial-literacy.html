Financial literacy for immigrants

A free online course on the Swedish financial system, built for newcomers to Sweden.

Did you know that you can negotiate your mortgage interest rate with your bank in Sweden? You can — and it can save a lot of money over time.

Newcomers to Sweden face real challenges understanding the Swedish financial system without the native knowledge that most locals take for granted. Together with colleagues at Lund and Aarhus universities, and in collaboration with Finansinspektionen (the Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority), I built an online course that explains the essentials in plain language.

What the course covers

  • Investment strategies and fund selection
  • The Swedish pension system
  • Retirement savings
  • How to buy a home in Sweden
  • How mortgages work and how to negotiate the rate
  • The impact of fees on long-run wealth
  • Video lectures, a glossary of Swedish financial terms, and discussion questions for your bank meeting

Take the course

The course is free and available in two languages:

  • finanskurs.se — Swedish version
  • sites.google.com/view/finanskurs-english — English version
